everyone and anyone who loves to play the difficult and strategic
game of pool. During the week we play in BU Central, in the basement
of GSU. We have scheduled meetings on Fridays or Saturdays. On Fridays
we meet from 3pm - 6pm at BU Central, and on Saturdays we reserve
tables at Jillian's Lucky Strike
